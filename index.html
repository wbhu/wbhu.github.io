About Me

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2022, supervised by Prof. WONG, Tien-Tsin. Before that I received my bachelor degree (Outstanding Graduates of Liaoning Province) in Computer Science and Engineering from Dalian University of Technology (DUT) in 2018, supervised by Prof. YANG, Xin. My research interests lie in the intersection between Computer Graphics and Computer Vision, especially the 3D Learning.
