About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. WONG, Tien-Tsin. Before that I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Dalian University of Technology (DUT) in 2018, supervised by Prof. YANG, Xin. My research interests include: Computer Graphics, Computer Vision and Deep Learning.
